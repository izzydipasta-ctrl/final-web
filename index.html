Meet the Strays

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017. Known for their powerful performances, self-produced music, and authentic lyrics, the group inspires fans worldwide with messages about individuality, resilience, and self-discovery. With members contributing to songwriting, composing, and producing, Stray Kids has built a unique sound that blends energetic hip-hop, electronic, and rock influences. Their fandom, called STAY, plays a huge role in their journey — representing the idea that Stray Kids and STAY always "stay" together through every challenge and success.
